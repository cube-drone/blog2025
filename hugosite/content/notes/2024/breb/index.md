### OVERNIGHT BREAD (+~5 hrs)

NIGHT:
* Mix the poolish:
* 500g flour
* 0.4g (1/8 tsp) yeast
* 500g water, 80F
* wait 12-14 hours (bubbly, triple volume)

DAY: (5 hrs)
* add 500g flour
* 21g salt
* 3g ( 3/4tsp ) yeast
* (you can mix dry ingredients separately first)
* 250g water, 105F
* mix
* timer: 3 hrs (2 folds)
* divide & proof: 1 hr
* oven: 475F, 30 minutes lid on, 15 minutes lid off
